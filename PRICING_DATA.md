# PRICING_DATA.md

# Overview

The pricing data used inside SpendPilot was collected manually from the official pricing pages of the respective AI tools. The pricing references were used to make the audit calculations and recommendations feel more realistic instead of using random placeholder values. All pricing values were verified manually during development and converted into INR where required for better consistency inside the application dashboard.

# Pricing Notes

- The application currently uses manually maintained pricing references.
- Pricing values may change over time depending on the official pricing updates from each provider.
- INR conversions were rounded for cleaner UI presentation and easier comparison inside the dashboard.
- The current version of SpendPilot focuses mainly on commonly used individual and team plans instead of full enterprise contract pricing.
